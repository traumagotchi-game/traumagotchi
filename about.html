Traumagotchi imagines a future where a person's trauma may be ejected from their mind/body and delegated to live in hyperspace — as their virtual pet.

The Traumagotchi can be fed, bathed, and rested. They tend to the DeepInTheMachineWorld TraumaCompostShrine.

Conceptually, Traumagotchi is an inquiry into the limits of computation. It asks: is it possible to task virtual beings with our psychological processes?

Practically, it is an exercise in witnessing the very personalized ways we relate to our own sensitive selves. And why-the-fuck not cast some healing spells on the interwebs?
